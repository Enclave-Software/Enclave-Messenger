How Ghost Secure Messenger Works

Ghost Secure Messenger uses your local network or an internet connection for communication, adding a robust layer of encryption to ensure your messages remain private.

Network-Based Communication

The application uses your existing network infrastructure (like Wi-Fi for LAN or your internet connection). This allows for flexible communication depending on your setup.

End-to-End Encryption

Messages are encrypted on the sender's device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient who also has the application and the correct keys. This ensures privacy over any network.
